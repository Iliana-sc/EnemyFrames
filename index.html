EnemyFrames

Description

EnemyFrames is an addon for World of Warcraft 1.12.1 that shows unit frames for enemy players. It's mainly focused around warsong gulch but also works in world pvp and other battlegrounds.

  • Shows HP/Mana/Rage of enemies
  • Shows up to 40 enemies at once
  • Easily select enemies from your UI
  • Flag carriers are marked with a flag icon
  • Easy configuration panel
  • Auto-leave battlegrounds
